ABOUT US

  V S R MACHINENI & Co. Chartered Accountants is a proprietorship firm in practice having Headquarters at Hyderabad, India. The firm was established in the year 2015.

  We at V S R MACHINENI & Co. maintain personal and long term relationship with clients to understand their needs to provide best possible service. Every member of the firm is encouraged to be friendly, professional and fully committed in delivering the best services.

  V S R MACHINENI & Co. is a professionally managed firm. The team consists of distinguished chartered accountants, corporate financial advisors and tax consultants. The firm represents a combination of specialized skills, which are geared to offers sound financial advice and personalized proactive services. Those associated with the firm have regular interaction with industry and other professionals which enables the firm to keep pace with contemporary developments and to meet the needs of its clients.

  We are empaneled auditors of various banks,having handled successfully assignments of various types of audit services with a value-added contribution for safeguarding banks interest, adding to the profitability of the bank by detecting substantial revenue leakages apart from statutory and regulatory compliances.
